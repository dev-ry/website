Who We Are

Prosper XO is an artist-first ethical technology company and cultural policy initiative, building the operating system for the creative economy.

We operate at the intersection of:

  • Public Education
  • Community-Led Cultural Programming
  • Policy Advocacy
  • Ethical Technology Development

Our work is grounded in lived artist experience, academic research, and civic collaboration. This deck focuses on what is already live and in motion today, and how sponsors and partners can support and grow alongside it.

Why This Matters

Artists power culture, but the systems around them extract, exploit, and silence them.

Music shapes identity, community, and local economies, yet the people who create it are often the least protected. Artists generate billions in cultural and economic value, while most independent musicians struggle to earn sustainable income. Mental health resources for creatives remain underfunded. Data, labor, and intellectual property are controlled by platforms, not creators, and the public has little visibility into how the industry truly operates.

Prosper XO exists to change this through education, cultural infrastructure, and ethical technology
