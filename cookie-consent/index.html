COOKIE CONSENT

Last updated: October 19, 2025

1. Overview

Prosper XO values transparency and user choice. This Cookie Consent page explains how cookies and similar technologies are used across prosperxo.com and related services.

At this time, Prosper XO does not use cookies or third-party tracking scripts. However, we maintain this page to provide clear information for when cookies or analytics tools are introduced in the future.

When that time comes, this page will describe:

  • the types of cookies we use,
  • why we use them,
  • how long they remain active,
  • and how you can control or delete them.

Until then, no cookies are being stored or accessed by our website.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device. They help sites remember your preferences, measure activity, and deliver personalized experiences. While cookies can improve functionality, Prosper XO is committed to using them responsibly and only when necessary to enhance user experience.

3. Future Use of Cookies

Prosper XO may use cookies or similar technologies in the future for purposes such as:

  • understanding how visitors engage with our site,
  • improving design and navigation,
  • maintaining session preferences or accessibility settings,
  • and ensuring secure website performance.

If we begin using cookies or analytics tools, we will:

  • clearly inform visitors on this Cookie Consent page and our Cookie Policy,
  • describe each category of cookie used (such as essential, performance, or analytics),
  • and request consent where legally required.

We will never use cookies for intrusive advertising or data resale.

4. How to Manage Cookies

When cookies become active on our website, you will be able to manage your preferences through a clear, unobtrusive consent tool and your browser settings.

Most browsers allow you to:

  • view or delete stored cookies,
  • block certain types of cookies,
  • or notify you before new cookies are placed.

Instructions for managing cookies can be found in your browser's help section.

5. Third-Party Services

If Prosper XO embeds or integrates tools from third-party providers in the future—such as videos, event forms, or analytics dashboards—those services may set their own cookies or tracking scripts. We will disclose these instances clearly and link to their respective policies.

6. Updates to This Page

We may update this Cookie Consent page as we expand our technology or features. Updates will always include a revision date at the top of this page. For broader updates to our data practices, please review our Privacy Policy.

7. Contact

For questions about this Cookie Consent page or Prosper XO's data practices, please contact: info@prosperxo.com

Summary

If we introduce cookies or analytics tools in the future, we'll notify visitors on this Cookie Consent page and our Cookie Policy, and update our Privacy Policy, to explain their purpose and use.
