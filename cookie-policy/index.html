COOKIE POLICY

Last updated: October 19, 2025

1. Overview

Prosper XO is committed to transparency and ethical data practices. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we may use them in the future, and how you can manage your preferences.

At this time, Prosper XO does not use cookies or any similar tracking technologies on prosperxo.com. This policy exists to outline how we will handle cookies if we introduce them later, so you always know exactly what to expect.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, improve usability, and provide information to the site owner about how visitors interact with content.

Cookies can perform a variety of functions, such as:

  • remembering your preferences or login status,
  • helping pages load faster,
  • collecting basic analytics data,
  • improving accessibility or layout consistency,
  • and ensuring security across sessions.

3. Types of Cookies We May Use in the Future

When Prosper XO adds cookie functionality, we may use the following categories of cookies, always limited to their intended purpose:

  • Essential Cookies – Necessary for the website to function correctly, such as maintaining secure connections or saving minimal preferences.
  • Performance Cookies – Used to understand how visitors engage with our site, such as which pages are most visited or how users navigate.
  • Functionality Cookies – Remember settings and preferences (such as display modes or regional selections) to enhance user experience.
  • Analytics Cookies – Collect anonymous, aggregate data to help us improve usability, measure engagement, and guide future development.

We will not use cookies for targeted advertising or any purpose that conflicts with our values of privacy and user trust.

4. How We Use Cookie Data

If cookies are implemented in the future, Prosper XO will use them solely to:

  • analyze aggregate, anonymous visitor behavior,
  • measure performance and site reliability,
  • enhance content relevance and accessibility,
  • and maintain secure sessions for features that require authentication.

We will not use cookie data to personally identify you, and we will never sell or share cookie information with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

5. Managing and Controlling Cookies

When cookies become active on our website, you will have full control over their use through both:

  • our Cookie Consent management tool, and
  • your own browser settings.

You can choose to:

  • allow all cookies,
  • disable non-essential cookies,
  • delete existing cookies from your browser,
  • or block cookies entirely.

Most browsers also allow you to set preferences for each website individually. For details, refer to your browser's support section.

6. Third-Party Cookies and Integrations

Some embedded features or third-party services, such as event tools, video players, or analytics providers, may set their own cookies. In such cases, we will:

  • clearly identify which services are using cookies,
  • provide links to their privacy or cookie policies,
  • and ensure that these services follow lawful and ethical data practices.

7. Retention and Security

Any cookies used by Prosper XO in the future will be stored only for as long as necessary to achieve their stated purpose. Expiration periods will vary by cookie type, and temporary cookies will automatically expire when you close your browser.

We will use secure hosting environments and HTTPS encryption to protect cookie data from unauthorized access or misuse.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in technology, regulation, or Prosper XO's internal practices. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how Prosper XO uses and manages cookies. For broader updates to our data practices, please review our Privacy Policy.

9. Contact

For questions or concerns regarding this Cookie Policy or any of Prosper XO's data practices, please contact: info@prosperxo.com

Summary

Prosper XO does not currently use cookies or tracking technologies. If we begin using them in the future, we will do so transparently, ethically, and with clear options for you to manage your privacy.
