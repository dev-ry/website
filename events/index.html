Creative Futures, Secure Foundations:
Cybersecurity Awareness Month

October 8, 2025 · Capital Factory in Austin, TX · ACL Room, 16th floor

Why Attend?

Cybersecurity isn't just for corporations; it's for creatives too.

What to Expect

This month, we’re bringing together innovators at the intersection of tech, music, and culture to explore how we can:

  • Protect artists’ data and intellectual property
  • Build secure, transparent foundations for the creative economy
  • Ensure artists and communities thrive in the digital age

Join Prosper XO, alongside artists, technologists, and culture leaders, for an inspiring night of conversation, networking, and community-building.


Featuring Guest Speakers:


Yasmin Abdi - Founder & CEO @ noHack | Ex-Snap, Google, Meta


Yasmin is a renowned technical advisor in security, privacy, and data protection. With over seven years at Snap, Meta, and Google, she co-founded Meemo, an AI-powered app acquired by Coinbase for $95M. The solutions she built now serve 850M users worldwide.  Today, as CEO of noHack, she is leading the fight to make cybersecurity accessible, transparent, and empowering for creators everywhere.

Nagavalli Medicharla - Musician | Chair, Austin Music Commission | Mayor’s Appointee, Arts Commission | Board Chair, EQ Austin | Former Tech Management @ Dell & Visa


Nagavalli is an acclaimed singer, composer, and promoter who bridges the worlds of culture and technology. Beyond her artistry, she brings deep leadership experience across music advocacy and tech management, serving as Chair of the Austin Music Commission, Mayor’s Appointee to the Arts Commission, and Board Chair of EQ Austin. Her career includes tech leadership roles at Dell and Visa.
