CODE OF CONDUCT

Last updated: October 19, 2025

1. Purpose

Prosper XO was created to build a fair, inclusive, and empowering creative ecosystem. Our mission depends on collaboration and mutual respect among artists, partners, and communities.
This Code of Conduct outlines the standards of behavior expected from everyone who engages with Prosper XO — whether online, at events, or through our affiliated programs.

We believe technology should strengthen creativity, not divide it. By participating in the Prosper XO community, you agree to uphold these values and foster a safe, supportive environment for others.

2. Core Principles

Prosper XO is guided by a simple framework:

  • Respect – Treat all participants with courtesy, empathy, and dignity.
  • Integrity – Communicate honestly and uphold high ethical standards.
  • Inclusion – Welcome diverse backgrounds, perspectives, and creative expressions.
  • Accountability – Take responsibility for your actions and their impact on others.

3. Expected Behavior

Everyone engaging with Prosper XO, including team members, artists, partners, and event participants, is expected to:

  • Show respect in communication, collaboration, and creative exchange.
  • Value differing perspectives and maintain professionalism even in disagreement.
  • Protect confidentiality when handling shared ideas, works, or sensitive information.
  • Use Prosper XO tools and platforms only for lawful, constructive purposes.
  • Follow any additional rules or guidelines specific to Prosper XO programs or events.

4. Unacceptable Behavior

Prosper XO does not tolerate any form of:

  • Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation based on race, gender, sexual orientation, age, disability, religion, or any other protected status.
  • Abusive language, personal attacks, or deliberate disruption of community spaces.
  • Unsolicited advertising, spam, or exploitation of the platform for non-collaborative purposes.
  • Violation of others' intellectual property rights.
  • Any action that compromises the safety, privacy, or well-being of another person.

5. Reporting and Response

If you experience or witness a violation of this Code of Conduct, please contact: info@prosperxo.com

All reports will be handled confidentially and reviewed promptly by Prosper XO leadership.
We will assess each situation with fairness and discretion. Actions may include a private warning, removal from events or programs, or other steps as deemed appropriate.

6. Responsibilities of Prosper XO

Prosper XO commits to:

  • maintaining safe and inclusive environments for all participants,
  • enforcing this Code consistently and without bias,
  • providing resources for education, mediation, and improvement,
  • and evolving this policy as our community grows.

7. Application

This Code applies to all Prosper XO spaces, including:

  • prosperxo.com and affiliated subdomains,
  • events, programs, or digital sessions hosted by Prosper XO,
  • community discussions, collaborations, and communications on official channels.

Prosper XO also expects partners and collaborators to maintain comparable standards of professionalism and respect when representing the brand or engaging with our community.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Code of Conduct periodically to reflect evolving community needs and best practices. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

9. Summary

Prosper XO stands for creativity, respect, and accountability.
By engaging with our platform or community, you contribute to a shared culture rooted in fairness, curiosity, and mutual growth. Together, we build technology that supports artistry — not at its expense.
