Resource of the Month:
Austin Federation of Musicians (AFM) Local 433

The Austin Federation of Musicians is the local chapter of the American Federation of Musicians of the U.S. and Canada. Founded in 1907, AFM Local 433 has a long history of organizing local musicians to improve wages, working conditions, and labor protections, while advocating for fair treatment across the music industry.

This is a critical resource for musicians navigating contracts, pay disputes, labor rights, and collective advocacy; especially in an increasingly precarious gig economy.
