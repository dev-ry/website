PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: October 19, 2025

1. Introduction and Philosophy

Prosper XO is built on the belief that technology should empower creativity, not exploit it. We design tools that help artists, brands, and communities collaborate ethically and sustainably.

To serve that mission, we sometimes collect limited information about how people interact with our website and related services. This information helps us understand what works, improve user experience, and build a platform that better serves the creative community.

We believe privacy, transparency, and consent are essential parts of ethical design. This Privacy Policy explains how Prosper XO collects, uses, and protects information about you. It applies to all Prosper XO websites, subdomains, and online services under the prosperxo.com domain.

By using our website, you agree to the terms described here. If we make significant changes, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above.

2. Key Terms

  • "Prosper XO," "we," "us," and "our" mean Prosper XO, Inc. (or its successors), the organization that owns and operates prosperxo.com and related properties.
  • "Platform" or "Services" refer to the Prosper XO website, tools, content, and digital services offered under the prosperxo.com domain or associated subdomains.
  • "You" and "your" mean any individual, group, or organization using Prosper XO's website, tools, or services, whether directly or on behalf of someone else.
  • "Personal information" or "personal data" means any information that identifies you or could reasonably be used to identify you, such as your name, email address, phone number, IP address, or other contact details.
  • "Usage data" means information automatically collected when you visit the site, such as your browser type, device, referring URL, time spent on pages, or campaign source (for example, through UTM tracking links).
  • "Cookies" are small text files stored on your device that allow websites to recognize your browser or remember certain preferences. Prosper XO currently does not use cookies but may in the future.
  • "Third parties" means any company or service provider that is not directly operated by Prosper XO, including hosting services, analytics tools, and communication platforms.
  • "This Policy" or "this Privacy Policy" refers to this entire document.

3. What This Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy covers how Prosper XO collects, uses, and protects information obtained through:

  • visits to prosperxo.com and its subdomains,
  • event registration forms, surveys, or contact forms hosted by Prosper XO,
  • QR codes and tracking URLs used in campaigns and marketing materials.

This Policy does not cover:

  • third-party sites linked from Prosper XO pages,
  • tools or platforms operated by external partners,
  • services that have their own privacy policies (for example, embedded video players or form services).

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

4. Information We Collect

Prosper XO collects information in two main ways: directly from you and automatically through standard web technologies.

Direct information you provide may include:

  • your name and email address when you contact us or sign up for updates,
  • responses submitted through forms or surveys,
  • event registration details if you participate in Prosper XO programs.

Automatically collected information may include:

  • browser type, device information, operating system, and language,
  • referral data such as UTM parameters that help us understand how visitors find the site,
  • anonymized metrics like page views or time on site collected by standard web infrastructure.

We do not currently collect sensitive personal data such as government IDs, payment information, or location tracking. If new tools are added later that collect such data, we will clearly update this policy.

5. How We Use the Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • understand how people discover and interact with Prosper XO,
  • measure the reach and effectiveness of campaigns,
  • improve website usability and performance,
  • respond to inquiries or provide support,
  • maintain security and prevent misuse.

We do not sell or rent your information to third parties. Data is used only for Prosper XO's legitimate operational and mission-driven purposes.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Prosper XO does not currently use cookies or other browser-based tracking scripts. However, we do use UTM parameters and referral links to measure aggregate engagement and campaign performance.

If we introduce cookies or analytics tools in the future, we'll notify visitors on our Cookie Consent and Cookie Policy pages, and update this policy, to explain their purpose and use.

You can always manage or delete cookies through your browser settings.

7. How We Share Information

Prosper XO may share limited data only when necessary to operate our services or comply with legal obligations. Examples include:

  • hosting providers or content delivery networks that keep our website running,
  • email or communication platforms used to send updates or event information,
  • analytics providers, if added in the future, used solely to improve functionality.

All third-party partners are required to handle data in accordance with applicable privacy laws and to use it only for the purpose of providing contracted services.

We will never sell your personal information or share it for advertising purposes.

8. Data Retention and Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including HTTPS encryption, limited access controls, and secure servers. While no system is completely immune to breaches, we take privacy and security seriously and respond promptly to potential risks.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. These may include the right to:

  • access the data we hold about you,
  • request corrections or deletions,
  • withdraw consent for optional communications,
  • request a copy of your data in portable format.

To exercise these rights or ask questions about this policy, contact us at info@prosperxo.com.

10. International Access

Prosper XO is based in the United States. By using our website, you understand that your data may be stored or processed in the U.S. or other countries with different data protection laws.

We comply with applicable data protection principles regardless of where information is processed.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect new practices or legal requirements. Changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly. If significant changes are made, we will provide additional notice where required.

12. Contact

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or Prosper XO's data practices, please contact: info@prosperxo.com

Summary

Prosper XO collects only what is necessary, uses it responsibly, and never sells or rents personal data. We believe technology can be both creative and ethical, and we are committed to keeping it that way.
